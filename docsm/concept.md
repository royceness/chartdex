## Product Concept

ChartDex has two spaces:

### Org Space
Shared dashboards visible to the organisation:
- Revenue Overview
- Checkout Funnel
- Campaign Performance

Users can:
- view org dashboards
- ask voice/text to open dashboards
- ask Codex to explain metrics
- ask Codex to add a panel to a dashboard
- promote useful personal dashboards into org space

### My Space
Personal exploratory dashboards created from ad hoc questions.

Users can ask:
- "How's the new experiment rollout going?"
- "What happened to Android checkout conversion?"
- "Why did revenue dip last week?"

ChartDex creates a personal dashboard, persists it, and shows a Codex investigation summary.
